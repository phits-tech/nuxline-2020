Hackathon

Each team must build and present a product made using LINE technologies, such as:

  • LINE Login
  • LINE Messaging API
  • LINE Frontend Framework (LIFF)
  • LINE Pay

You may begin work on your product prior to the hackathon weekend. It can be an entirely new product, or an extension of an existing product. During the weekend you will have approximately 8 hours to extend and improve your product, in addition to guidance from our team of experts & mentors.

Rules

  • Your product must use at least one LINE technology
  • Your product cannot have been previously entered into another hackathon/contest
  • Your product must be the sole work of your team
